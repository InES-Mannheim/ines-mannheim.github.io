Description

Process Mining
In The Cloud

This tool is developed as a part of a Master Team Project at the University of Mannheim with collaboration of HoneyWell's Movilizer Mobile Enterprise Application Platform for developing mobile business models. The tool allows you to import data with several formats including: BPMN, XES and Movilizer's Movelet. You can visualize the process model as a whole or you can inspect chunks of it through the Variants view. The colouring of nodes as well as the thickness and labeling of the edges allows you to identify critical activities and path in a variant. You can also filter the process with some KPIs including: Processing Time and Process Steps.

Features

Powerful architecture
built based on SPA

The web application is developed to harness the power of SPA – Smart Process API. The main aim to use SPA was because it helped translate the several formats with which processes are expressed and created a mapping between these formats; thus, integrating them. SPA acts as our database where all the input files, after preprocessing (if needed), are put into. The SPA uses the generated BPMN and XES files needed for our tool visualization and converts them to RDF Models.

Data Import
With Multiple Formats

Our tool provides you with the flexibility of importing your own processes in several formats. Whether you develop a Movelet in the Movilizer Cloud or you want to import your own process model in BPMN or XES format, Movilizer web application handles different formats to make your analysis easier.

Flexible Process Visualization
Process versus Variant

With the flexibility to switch between complete Process Model view and Variant view of your process, the tool gives you the opportunity to explore fragments of your process and analyze them carefully. You can also select specific variant of the process to inspect critical paths and activities within a unique path of your process.

Performance Analysis
With KPIs Filtering

From Processing Time to Process Steps, you can use the different KPIs to filter your process. Using these KPIs to investigate and assess your process, you can better understand your process and detect bottlenecks in the process.
